ABOUT US


MISSION

Brindar una amplia gama de fundas para celulares a precios asequibles, con el objetivo de proteger, decorar, personalizar, complementar y mejorar la experiencia de cada uno de nuestros clientes a la hora utilizar su teléfono móvil. Agregando valor brindando un excelente servicio al cliente y servicio post-venta, con procesos eficientes, mediante un grupo humano identificado y calificado con la cultura de servicio al cliente. Así logrando la rentabilidad que nos permita sostenernos en el tiempo y asumiendo un compromiso total con nuestros clientes, productos y empleados.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

• Do you have vegan and/or vegetarian options?

Yes, the amount of options we currently have is not the same as the non-vegan ones, we proud ourselves for having a lot of them.

• Do I need to make a reservation?

No, while our café is considered one of the fancier ones around the area, a reservation is no necessary.

• Do you offer takeout and delivery?

We offer takeout but not delivery services.

• Is Sweet Cafe good for kids?

Yes. We have a few options that kids usually like, high chairs available, coloring books and right next to the establishment there's a playground for the olders kids.

• Do you sell any type of alcohol?

No, we do not sell alcohol. That would mean our cafe is also not suitable for Happy Hours.

• Are there parking lots nearby?

Yes, for cars, bikes and motocycles.
